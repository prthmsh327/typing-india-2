HOW TO IMPROVE YOUR TYPING SPEED

Improving typing speed takes consistent daily practice, not marathon sessions. 20 minutes every day is more effective than 2 hours once a week. Here's a proven progression plan:

Week 1–2: Focus only on accuracy. Slow down until you make fewer than 3 errors per minute. Speed will come naturally once your fingers know where keys are.

Week 3–4: Introduce timed tests. Use 1-minute tests and push your speed slightly beyond your comfort zone each session.

Month 2–3: Move to 5-minute tests. Longer tests build endurance and expose different weaknesses than short tests.

Month 3+: Use Hard mode and punctuation tests. Real-world typing involves numbers, symbols, and punctuation — not just common words.
