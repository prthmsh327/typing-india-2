FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How is this different from other typing test websites?
Most sites just test general typing speed. We simulate specific government exams — CPCT uses a penalty formula where each error deducts 0.5 WPM. Our CPCT Simulator applies this exact formula so you know your real score before the actual exam. No other free site does this for Indian government exams.
Which exam should I prepare for first?
Start with CPCT if you're in Madhya Pradesh — it's required for most MP state government jobs. For central government jobs (SSC), use our SSC simulator. Check our Government Jobs page for state-specific guidance on UP, Rajasthan, and other states.
How long does it take to reach 30 WPM?
Most beginners who practice 20–30 minutes daily reach 30 WPM in English within 6–8 weeks. Hindi typing takes longer — typically 3–4 months to reach 20 WPM from scratch because you also need to learn the Inscript keyboard layout simultaneously.
Do I need to install any software?
No. Everything runs in your browser. For Hindi typing practice however, you do need to set up the Inscript keyboard layout in Windows settings — go to Settings → Time & Language → Language → Add Hindi → Enable Inscript keyboard. This is free and built into Windows.
