PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: May 2025

YOUR DATA

TypingTestIndia runs entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit your typed text to any server. Your typing test results (best WPM) are stored locally in your browser using localStorage and are never sent to us.

We use Google Analytics to understand aggregate traffic (page views, device types, general location). This is anonymized and used only to improve the site.
